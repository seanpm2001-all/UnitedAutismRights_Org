United Autism Rights organization - homepage

Welcome to the homepage for the UAR!

I started a movement for the rights of people with Autism, as I myself have Autism, and there are no good mainstream Autism rights organizations.

This project will be maintained by people with Autism, so that the articles don't get put into an incorrect perception.

Articles are currently split into 3 categories:

Autism Rights

Common sense (common sense and Autism)

The Spectrum (explaining various elements of the Autism spectrum)

More articles will be added in the future.
