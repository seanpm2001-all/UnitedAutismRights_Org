Hiring - UAR/Hiring

THe UAR is currently hiring volunteers for writing articles, and spreading word of the Autism Spectrum. Currently, only people with Autism can be hired for writing jobs, other jobs can be held by anyone, regardless of if they have Autism or not. I don't want the articles to be put into the wrong perspective and pass through the wrong filters.

Currently, no paid positions are available.

This article is a stub. You can help UAR by expanding it!

Some notes:

The puzzle piece is an outdated symbol for Autism, from a 2018 study, it evokes a bad reaction [1A]
