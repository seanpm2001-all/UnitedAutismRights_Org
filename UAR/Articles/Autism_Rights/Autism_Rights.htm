Autism Rights - UAR/Autism_Rights

People with Autism should have all the same rights as someone who doesn't have Autism. Depending on the Spectrum, there really isn't much difference between people with and without Autism. We are still human. Some people who are on the high end of the spectrum can blend in to society well, and sometimes outperform others, with the con of a few/several social/behavioral issues.

Some notes:

The puzzle piece is an outdated symbol for Autism, from a 2018 study, it evokes a bad reaction [1A]
