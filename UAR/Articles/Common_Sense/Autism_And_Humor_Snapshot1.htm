Autism and Humor - UAR/Common_Sense

Just because someone has Autism doesn't mean you can't be humourous with them. Telling Autism jokes to people with Autism is perfectly fine, as long as it isn't done as an insult, and doesn't turn into bullying. Dark humor is fine from time to time. Just make sure the person you are joking with is OK with it.

Some notes:

The puzzle piece is an outdated symbol for Autism, from a 2018 study, it evokes a bad reaction [1A]
